Site Purpose

The Harvest Hills Neighbors site purpose is to be a one stop shop for the members of the Harvest Hills neighborhood. Not only can they get a quick view at the weather, but members would be able to see community updates or announcements.

This could be different activities being hosted in the neighborhood, like a neighborhood wide garage sale or announcements about pool maintenance or sports teams sign ups. It could also be a place for members to post questions or concerns, get connected with other members, or make important posts such as posting that a pet was lost.
