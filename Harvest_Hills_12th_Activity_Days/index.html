The Purpose of Activity Days

Discover

Every child has unique, divine talents, meant to bless their lives, and the lives of those around them. These talents are not always obvious or known, and sometimes take effort to discover. The Activity Days program seeks to expose these hidden talents through diverse activities, while also building faith in and knowledge of the Gospel, and developing a love for selfless service.

Plan

Everything was spiritual before it was physical, and every talent discovered requires a plan to allow it to floruish and bloom. Children and parents should create meaningful plans and goals to further develop talents. There are many resources available to help plan that can be found among our list of resources below.
