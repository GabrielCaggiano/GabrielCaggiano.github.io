Design Principles

White Space, Hick's Law, and The Rule of Thirds

White Space

  • Quip
  • https://www.getquip.com/

I chose the quip website for white space because I liked how the empty space was used to separate the different elements.

You can particularly see this in the menu bar. Even in the image, the background and foreground help to divide things and it creates a satisfying layout.

Hick's Law

  • Linked In
  • https://www.linkedin.com/

Hick's Law is about making choosing easier, at least for good design it is. I felt linked In did a great job making the choice easy. You have just four choices: Sign In, find a job, find a person, or learn a skill.

All these things are pertinent to anyone who is looking for employment or to better their career.

The Rule of Thirds

  • STAAK
  • https://www.staak.co.uk/

The rule of thirds comes from art, and art is typically more aesthetically pleasing when the image follows the rule of thirds.

Staak does a good job with this. You can clearly tell this image followst the rule of thirds with the man in the left third and the car taking up the two right thirds.
