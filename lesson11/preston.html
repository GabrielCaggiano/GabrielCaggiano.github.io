Goodbye Toby!

Toby Flenserdon, the most infamous citizen of Preston, and the most annoying turn light inspector in town, is finally moving away. Toby is headed to Costa Rica, in search of who knows what. All that anyone cares about is that he is finally gone. Since he moved to the town and implemented the turn light inspection enforcement, life has been just a little worse for everyone in town. One person we interviewed said, “He’s okay I guess but he's always going on about how it’s very important we all make sure our turn signals are working. Mine work but I don’t use them out of spite.”

“I just wanted to make people safe and encourage people to actually use their signals, no one uses them here, and if often causes accidents.” said Toby, but no one really cares. Mayor Scott has declared today a new town holiday, and has announced that he will be throwing a huge going away party for Toby, but of course, Toby is not invited.
